|About|


Hello, welcome to my online gallery.
My name is Benjamin Yu. I created this gallery for the purpose of sharing projects I make and thoughts I have, while also recording my progress in learning.
I have always been deeply interested in art and creative activities. I am particularly interested in combining art with technology for entertainment, educational and problem-solving purposes. 
I believe that through knowledge accumulation and deep reflection, we can enhance our abilities to view the world and everything within it with a more complete view. To me, creation is an interesting, meaningful and helpful way to apply my knowledge and thoughts.
In this gallery, you can find illustration, photography and programming projects I've worked on. I hope you will find your experience here enjoyable.
Thank you for visiting.

by Benjamin Tse-Bung Yu, 2019
